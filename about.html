My Story...

Journey to becoming a full stack developer

In 2010 my dad gifted me my first computer, his old 2008 era imac. The only problem is my dad had absolutely fried the thing before he had gave it to me. Hard drives were damaged from a rigerous apartment move, the motherboard had a chipped display port from an initial failed attempt at installing a new hard drive, and the cd drive was stuck due to the prongs being abused from a previous modification. Pair that all together with the warranty being ruined from all the tampering and you have an adventerous summer in learning to scrap together your first working computer.

Recently rekindling my childhood fondness for computers, I decided to enroll in a Coding Bootcamp course at UT. Now armed with a fresh batch of new skills I'm excited to embark on a new path with endless posibilites!
